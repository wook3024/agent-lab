# Complex Brief Checklist

Use this checklist before finalizing the brief.

- The goal changes one product behavior or one tightly related cluster.
- The brief says why the task is complex.
- Done conditions are observable and testable.
- Non-goals fence off tempting adjacent work.
- Required files, docs, and tests are called out.
- Risk level is explicit.
- Approval requirements are explicit.
- Reviewer focus points are explicit.
